Korean Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

Prepare to be MELTED by this?? Introducing the best Korean fried chicken recipe with melted cheese!!!

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Cheese
  • White sugar
  • Oregano
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Stir together sugar, oregano, garlic, tomato sauce.
  2. Toss chicken with bread crumbs
  3. Deep fry the chicken
  4. Add cheese and add the sauce.
