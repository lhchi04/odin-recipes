Cheesecake Recipe

Description

I know your obsession with cheesecake, so why don't make one?
I failed 3 times but don't give up because this is the simplest recipe I found!

Ingredients

  • Crackers
  • Cream cheese (room temperature)
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Crush crackers with butter and bake the crust.
  2. Mix cream cheese until it's smooth then add sugar, vanilla extract, and eggs (one at a time).
  3. Bake in water bath, let the cheesecake cool for at least an hour.
